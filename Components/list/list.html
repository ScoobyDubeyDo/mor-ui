List

Lists are a continuous group of text or images. They are composed of items containing primary and supplemental actions, which are represented by icons and text. Lists can be used at so many places, navigation bar, stacked notifications, article pages, etc.

Imports

Horizontal List

A horizontal list is used when you want to list chips or tabs etc.

Vertical List

All your stacked components like notifications, article pages, etc. will be created using a vertical list.
