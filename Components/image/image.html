Image

An image is a graphic representation of something. They can be responsive to fit the width of the parent (so that they don't jump out of their parent container), And they can also be customized for round shape..

Imports

Responsive Image

The height of the image will be adjusted taking into account the same aspect ratio. If you would like to modify the aspect ratio, you will need to crop the image.

Responsive Round Image

Image that will grow to 100% width of its parent container and will also be responsive. The height of the image will be adjusted keeping the same aspect ratio.

Round does not mean a perfect circle, it is dependent upon the aspect ratio of the image.
